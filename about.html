Our Identity

Built on Rock.
Driven by Passion.

JVR Enterprises was founded with a singular vision — to bring the finest natural stone from Tamil Nadu's quarries to the world. Headquartered in Madurai, we have grown from a regional contractor to a trusted name in granite exports, quarry management, and government engineering projects.

Our operations span the entire granite value chain: from securing and licensing quarry sites, conducting environmentally responsible extraction, to processing premium slabs and delivering them to clients across India and globally.

We hold all necessary environmental clearances from the Tamil Nadu Government and operate under strict safety and sustainability protocols.
